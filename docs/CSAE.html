MMIW - Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Safety Standards

Last updated: 07/13/2025

Our Commitment to Child Safety

At MMIW, protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) is a foundational part of our safety framework. We are committed to ensuring our platform remains a safe space for users of all ages and that our systems actively detect and prevent any form of CSAE.

This document outlines the measures, policies, and procedures we've implemented to comply with Google Play's CSAE safety standards and global best practices.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

MMIW has a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes:

  • Sharing, uploading, or soliciting sexually explicit content involving minors.
  • Grooming or attempting to groom minors.
  • Engaging in or facilitating inappropriate adult-minor interactions.

Violations result in immediate account suspension, reporting to relevant authorities, and permanent bans from our platform.

2. User Reporting and Moderation Tools

We empower our community to report abuse with the following tools:

  • In-app reporting: At the end of the experience, users can report any CSAE-related content or interactions.
  • Fast moderation: Reports are reviewed promptly.
  • Appeals system: Users can appeal decisions, but CSAE-related reports receive no tolerance.

3. Proactive Detection Measures

We review interactions and content shared with MMIW for potential CSAE indicators.

4. Collaboration With Authorities

We will collaborate with any organizations and authorities including:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • INHOPE global network of hotlines
  • Law enforcement agencies in relevant jurisdictions

We promptly report all CSAE-related incidents and provide necessary data to assist investigations.

5. Contact Information for CSAE Concerns

If you suspect child sexual abuse or exploitation on our platform, please report it immediately:

  • Email: mehequanna@gmail.com
  • Escalation Team: Our CSAE response team reviews all reports within 48 hours.

6. Enforcement and Policy Updates

We continuously improve our CSAE prevention policies based on:

  • Law enforcement feedback
  • User reporting trends
  • Regulatory requirements
  • Industry best practices

Policy violations lead to permanent bans and legal escalation as required.

Thank you for helping us maintain a safe environment for all users.
